About Me

In the dynamic world of design and technology, I'm your friendly neighborhood UX designer with a solid two years of experience under my belt. My journey in the field of User Experience design has been nothing short of an exhilarating adventure, filled with endless curiosity and an unyielding desire for self-improvement.


I kicked off my career with a vision - to build a strong connection between design and development. This led me to the exciting path of learning coding. By adding front-end development skills to my toolkit, I've been able to create a special bond between design and code. This dual expertise has not only enriched my understanding of the complex coding world but has also empowered me to deliver user-centric, technically sound, and visually captivating solutions in my UX design work.


My commitment to continuous learning and professional growth is a cornerstone of my professional identity. I am confident that my journey as a UX designer and front-end developer is just beginning, and I eagerly anticipate further opportunities to refine and apply my skills in creating innovative, user-centric solutions in the ever-evolving landscape of design and technology.
